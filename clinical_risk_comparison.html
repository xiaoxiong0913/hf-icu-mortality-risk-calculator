Model scope

The calculator accepts the 10 first-day variables retained in the manuscript model: age, sex, systolic blood pressure, mean arterial pressure, heart rate, oxygen saturation, blood urea nitrogen, creatinine, bicarbonate, and arterial pH. The page is intended for research review of the manuscript model and benchmark comparison.
